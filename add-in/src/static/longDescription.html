AI Writing Assistant Add-in

Unlock your full writing potential with the AI Writing Assistant Add-in! Designed to integrate seamlessly with text editors like Microsoft Word, this add-in leverages cutting-edge AI to help you write smarter, faster, and with confidence.

Key Features

Reflections for Personalized Feedback

  • Receive detailed, tailored feedback on your writing.
  • Use pre-built prompts or create customized prompts to suit your specific writing goals.
  • Identify areas for improvement in tone, style, and structure.

QVE (Quick Writing Enhancement)

Transform your writing workflow with these smart tools:

  • Generate new sentences based on your context to expand your ideas.
  • Predict questions inspired by your content to enhance clarity or address potential reader inquiries.
  • Receive recommendations for the next word, phrase, or rhetorical move to maintain coherence.
  • Easily adjust the focus area in your document to refine AI suggestions specific to your needs.

Chat: Your Personal AI Writing Consultant

  • Engage with AI to explore ideas, refine sentences, and brainstorm creative solutions.
  • Ask customized questions or request tailored suggestions.
  • Receive explanations, alternative phrasing, or new angles for your content.

Multi-Platform Login

  • Log in seamlessly with Google, Microsoft, or Facebook accounts.
  • Create a new account directly within the app for added convenience.

Privacy and Security

  • Your document content is processed in real-time and not permanently stored.
  • We collect only anonymized usage data to improve features and functionality.
  • Participation in research is entirely optional and subject to strict consent guidelines.
  • All usage data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with industry standards.

How to Use

  1. Install the Add-in from the Microsoft Store.
  2. Log in using Google, Microsoft, Facebook, or a new account.
  3. Start writing and highlight text or enter a custom prompt to receive instant feedback and suggestions.
  4. Customize features such as AI focus or mode to suit your specific needs.
