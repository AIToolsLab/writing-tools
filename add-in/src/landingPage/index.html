Frequently Asked Questions

  • arrow_drop_downWhat is the purpose of the tool?
    The tool is designed to assist writers in their writing process by promoting reflection and encouraging revision of their writing. It uses large language models to generate feedback on the writer's text, providing them with opportunities to reflect on their work and make meaningful revisions.
  • arrow_drop_downHow does the tool work?
    The tool provides an interactive user interface that displays reflection cards. These cards can either contain a summary of important ideas in the text or non-directive feedback generated by the large language model. By enabling writers to interact with these cards, the tool aims to help them reflect on their writing and make original discoveries that result in meaningful revisions.
  • arrow_drop_downHow can I use the tool?
    The tool is developed as a Microsoft Word add-in. It includes predefined prompts that users can select to generate summary texts or non-directive feedback. Users can also customize prompts to better meet their needs through the prompt editor. Highlighted cards indicate the current paragraph that the user is revising.
